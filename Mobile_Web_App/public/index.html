IBM Watson IoT Platform Example App

Congratulations, you have deployed the IBM Watson IoT Platform example application!

If you used the Deploy to Bluemix button to get here, then you already have an IBM Watson IoT Platform organization created and ready to receive your data. Here's how to get started:

Step 1: Connect your Smartphone

With your smartphone, visit the following link:    

On your smartphone, you'll be prompted to enter a name for your device and to generate a new passcode.

Step 2: View your data

Access your IBM Watson IoT Platform dashboard to see the smartphone data.

From your deployed IBM Watson IoT Platform service in Bluemix:

  1. Click the Launch Dashboard button
  2. Go to the devices tab
  3. Select your smartphone to see the data it is streaming

Step 3: Extend the application

From there you can do a number of things to move towards a more complete IoT solution:

  1. Follow a recipe to connect another type of IoT device
  2. Extend this application to get data from your IBM Watson IoT Platform service through the available APIs
  3. Deploy and bind the IoT Real-Time Insights service to create custom dashboards and real-time data alerts
